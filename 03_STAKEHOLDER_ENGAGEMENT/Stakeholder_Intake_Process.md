## Pre-Meeting Preparation Procedures
1. Review project objectives and stakeholder roles.
2. Prepare agenda and distribute to stakeholders.
3. Collect necessary documentation related to the project.

## 45-60 Minute Intake Interview Structure
### Opening
- Introduction and purpose of the meeting.
- Set agenda and expectations.

### Current State
- Discuss the current state of the project and any previous work done.

### Risk Identification
- Identify potential risks and issues related to the project.

### Resource Constraints
- Discuss resource availability and constraints that may impact the project.

## Assessment Questionnaires for IT Operations and Clinical Departments
- IT Operations:
  1. What technologies are currently in use?
  2. Are there any existing compliance issues?

- Clinical Departments:
  1. How do current practices align with compliance requirements?
  2. What challenges do you face?

## Cross-Functional Coordination Frameworks
- **Legal Team:** Review compliance and regulatory issues.
- **IT Team:** Ensure all technological aspects meet compliance standards.
- **Clinical Team:** Address patient care and clinical safety.
- **Finance Team:** Assess financial implications of compliance.
- **HR Team:** Manage staffing and training related to compliance.

## Real MediGlobal Case Studies on Vendor Breach and Policy Conflicts
- Case Study 1: Overview of vendor breach incident.
- Case Study 2: Analysis of policy conflict and resolution steps taken.
