# Internal Audit Program for MediGlobal HealthTech Inc.

## Overview
This Internal Audit Program outlines the procedures, responsibilities, and timelines for conducting audits in alignment with the ISO 27001 audit framework. It aims to enhance compliance, ensure data security, and improve overall organizational effectiveness.

## 1. ISO 27001 Audit Framework
- **Purpose:** To assess the effectiveness of the Information Security Management System (ISMS).
- **Key Areas:** Security Policies, Risk Assessment, Security Controls, Internal Audits.

## 2. Evidence Collection Procedures
- **Documentation:** Identify and collect relevant documentation including policies, procedures, and records.
- **Interviews:** Conduct interviews with staff to gather evidence about the implementation of security controls.
- **Observations:** Observe processes and practices to ensure compliance with documented procedures.

## 3. Common Healthcare Findings
- **Lack of Documentation:** Insufficient records of security training and incidents.
- **Weak Access Controls:** Inadequate controls governing who can access sensitive information.
- **Data Breaches:** Instances of unauthorized disclosures of patient data.

## 4. Audit Checklists
- **General Audit Checklist:**
  - [ ] Review of ISMS policies.
  - [ ] Verification of access control mechanisms.
  - [ ] Assessment of incident management procedures.

- **ISO 27001 Checklist:**
  - [ ] Compliance with risk assessment requirements.
  - [ ] Review of security control implementation.
  - [ ] Evaluation of monitoring and measurement processes.

## 5. Corrective Action Procedures
- **Identification of Non-Conformities:** Document findings from audits and categorize them.
- **Corrective Action Plan:** Develop a plan that includes responsibilities and timelines for remediation.
- **Follow-up:** Schedule follow-ups to ensure corrective actions have been implemented and are effective.

## 6. Audit Timeline
- **Quarterly Audits:** Schedule audits on a quarterly basis to ensure continuous compliance and improvement.
  - **Month 1:** Plan and prepare.
  - **Month 2:** Conduct fieldwork and evidence collection.
  - **Month 3:** Prepare audit report and present findings.
  - **Month 4:** Review and follow-up on corrective actions.

## Conclusion
This Internal Audit Program is vital for sustaining compliance with ISO 27001 standards and enhancing the security posture of MediGlobal HealthTech Inc. Regular audits will help in identifying areas for improvement and ensuring effective risk management throughout the organization.
