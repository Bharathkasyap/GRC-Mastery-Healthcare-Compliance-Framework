# Ransomware Attack Management Plan

## Detection and Containment Procedures
- Continuous monitoring for unusual activity.
- Use of endpoint detection and response (EDR) tools.

## Immediate Response Actions
- Alert IT security team.
- Initiate incident response protocols.

## System Isolation Protocols
- Disconnect affected systems from the network.
- Prevent further spread by isolating critical systems.

## Backup System Activation
- Verify backups are intact and functional.
- Restore systems from the latest clean backup.

## Incident Command Structure
- Establish an incident command team.
- Assign roles and responsibilities.

## Law Enforcement Coordination (FBI)
- Contact FBI Cyber Crime Division.
- Document all findings for legal purposes.

## Ransom Decision-Making Process
- Evaluate the impact of the ransomware.
- Decide whether to negotiate or restore from backups.

## Business Continuity Activation
- Activate business continuity plans to minimize downtime.
- Ensure essential functions can continue operations.

## Patient Notification Procedures
- Inform affected patients about potential data breaches.
- Provide guidance on identity protection services.

## Regulatory Reporting Requirements
- Report incidents to healthcare regulatory bodies.
- Comply with HIPAA breach notification rules.

## Recovery Timeline
- Estimated recovery time: 24-48 hours for full system restoration.

## System Hardening Post-Recovery
- Apply security patches and updates.
- Review access controls and permissions.

## Real Healthcare Ransomware Case Study
- Analysis of a recent ransomware attack on a healthcare provider in 2021.
- Financial impact of approximately $5 million due to system downtime and data breach penalties.
