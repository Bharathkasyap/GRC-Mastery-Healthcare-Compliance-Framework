# Risk Assessment Report

## Healthcare-Specific Risks

1. **PHI Data Breach**  
   **Financial Impact:** $15-50M HIPAA exposure  
   **Likelihood:** High  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Implement strict access controls.  
   - Conduct regular employee training on data privacy.
   - Regular audits and compliance checks.
   
2. **EHR Downtime**  
   **Financial Impact:** Varies based on operational disruption  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Maintain backup systems.  
   - Implement load balancing for EHR systems.
   
3. **Vendor Compromise**  
   **Financial Impact:** Varies  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Conduct thorough vendor assessments before contracts.  
   - Enforce contractual security standards.
   
4. **Ransomware Attacks**  
   **Financial Impact:** Can exceed $1M depending on recovery  
   **Likelihood:** High  
   **Impact:** Very High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**
   - Regular backups.  
   - Employee training and awareness programs.
   
5. **Insider Threats**  
   **Financial Impact:** Varies  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Implement user activity monitoring.  
   - Restrict data access on a need-to-know basis.
   
6. **Regulatory Audit Failures**  
   **Financial Impact:** Fines and operational restrictions  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Conduct regular internal audits.  
   - Maintain updated policies and training.
   
7. **Medical Device Vulnerabilities**  
   **Financial Impact:** Varies  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Perform risk assessments on devices.  
   - Ensure devices are regularly patched.
   
8. **Business Continuity Failures**  
   **Financial Impact:** Can result in critical service disruptions  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Comprehensive business continuity planning and testing.
   
9. **Supply Chain Disruption**  
   **Financial Impact:** Varies by dependency  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Diversifying suppliers and stockpiling critical supplies.
   
10. **Data Integrity Issues**  
   **Financial Impact:** Can lead to patient safety issues and legal challenges  
   **Likelihood:** Medium  
   **Impact:** High  
   **Mitigation Strategies:**  
   - Regular data validation protocols.
