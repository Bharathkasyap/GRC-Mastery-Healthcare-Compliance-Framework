# Governance Framework

This comprehensive governance framework outlines the essential policies and procedures necessary for robust healthcare compliance and security. It includes the following elements:

## 1. Core Policies
- Policy 1: Data Integrity
- Policy 2: Privacy and Confidentiality
- Policy 3: Incident Response
- Policy 4: Risk Management
- Policy 5: Access Control
- Policy 6: Compliance Monitoring
- Policy 7: Training and Awareness
- Policy 8: Third-party Management

## 2. ISO 27001 Annex A Mapping
Mapping of 93 controls from ISO 27001 Annex A to our governance framework ensures compliance with international standards.

## 3. HIPAA Security Rule Alignment
A detailed analysis aligning our policies with the HIPAA Security Rule (45 CFR) to ensure patient privacy and data security.

## 4. Policy Development Process
This policy development process is structured in five phases with a 70-day timeline:
- Phase 1: Assessment
- Phase 2: Development
- Phase 3: Review
- Phase 4: Approval
- Phase 5: Implementation

## 5. Real-world Case Studies
Incorporating case studies from MediGlobal to illustrate the application of policies in real-world scenarios.

## 6. Network Architecture Diagrams
Visual representations of the network architecture to facilitate understanding and compliance.

## 7. Data Classification Matrices
Matrices detailing data classification levels to ensure appropriate handling and security.

## 8. Vendor Risk Assessment Framework
A comprehensive framework assessing risks for 847 vendors to safeguard sensitive information.

## 9. Security Training Tiers
Establishing different tiers of security training based on roles and responsibilities.

## 10. GRC Interview Preparation Questions
A curated list of interview questions to prepare for Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) discussions.

This document serves as a foundational resource for establishing a compliant and secure healthcare environment.
