# Incident Response Plan

## Purpose
The purpose of this Incident Response Plan is to outline
how the organization will prepare for, detect, respond to,
and recover from any incidents that may disrupt normal operations.

## Scope
This plan applies to all employees, contractors,
and third-party service providers handling sensitive information.

## Response Phases
1. **Preparation**: Train team members and establish communication
   protocols.
2. **Detection and Analysis**: Monitor and analyze incidents to assess
the impact.
3. **Containment**: Implement measures to limit the impact of the incident.
4. **Eradication**: Remove the cause of the incident.
5. **Recovery**: Restore systems and processes to normal operations.
6. **Post-Incident Review**: Analyze the response to improve future
   incident reactions.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Incident Response Team**: Responsible for managing
   the response and recovery process.
- **IT Staff**: Support technical aspects of incident handling.
- **Management**: Provide oversight and resources.
